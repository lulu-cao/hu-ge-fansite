Hu Ge Fan Site

Hu Ge (1982- ) is a Chinese actor and singer. He played the leading role Li Xiaoyao in Chinese Paladin in 2005 and immediately skyrocketed to fame as one of China's most popular actors. He received the Best Actor Award at the 22nd Magnolia Awards and the Golden Eagle Awards for Nirvana in Fire. He ranked 24th on the Forbes China Celebrity 100 List for 2020.
